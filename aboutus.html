The way we are able to produce our products is through purchasing the extra items produced by the manufactoreres from larger company orders for a small markup of what they are actually work. We then resell them based off of the market and the demand for it. To help lower our prices even more the shipping takes approxametly 2 weeks to 1 month. This lets us help out our customers with affordable high end products.
